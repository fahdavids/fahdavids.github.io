Founded in 2018, 'u' is Cape Town-based fashion label designing streetwear for womxn.


The brand was born out of a necessity for women’s streetwear. There weren’t many females doing women’s streetwear at the time and for me it was important because I was a streetwear gyal :)

Most women’s clothing today does not cater for modest women or comfort and for me, comfort is super important. Each garment is created with that in mind and I wanted to highlight that covering up doesn’t mean you can’t be hella stylish and stunt :)
